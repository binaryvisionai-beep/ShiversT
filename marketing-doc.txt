Some places are built with plans. Ours was built with feeling.
Four rooms. One soul. A story that existed long before we ever thought to give it a name.
The Beginning
There was never a grand blueprint spread across a polished table. No calculated vision of what the future should become. There were only hands — patient hands — shaping clay into walls, restoring old wood that already carried memories, placing objects not for decoration, but because they belonged.
Shivers did not emerge like a business. It unfolded the way a home does — slowly, instinctively, lovingly. A place shaped over time by people who believed that beauty should feel lived in, never manufactured.
“We never designed rooms. We lived with them quietly, until they revealed what they wished to become and so Oasis came into being. Not as a property. Not as a product but as a sanctuary where every corner carries the imprint of the same hands that prepared the meals, swept the courtyard, lit the lamps at dusk, and welcomed strangers as though they had been expected all along.
Four Rooms · Four States of Being
We have only four rooms. People often ask why we never built more.
The answer is simple: some things lose their soul the moment they are multiplied. Each room at Oasis was created like a letter written to someone deeply cherished — slowly, intimately, with intention in every detail.
Every texture was chosen with care. Every object carries a history. The rustic art that lines the walls was never sourced for aesthetic appeal alone — it was discovered, inherited, preserved, remembered.

The Table at the Heart of It All
Before the rooms, there was always the table.
At Shivers, we have long believed that the sincerest form of hospitality begins with a meal.
Not performance. Not presentation. Not carefully rehearsed luxury.
Simply honest food — prepared from ingredients grown nearby, cooked slowly by people who care deeply about what arrives on your plate.
“We cook the way we built Oasis — patiently, from scratch, with our whole hearts in it.”
The restaurant was never created as a service. It is an invitation.
To sit longer than intended. To let conversations, wander. To allow the evening to unfold at its own pace.
What “Shivers” Means
The name was never about the cold.
It was chosen for that fleeting, unforgettable sensation — the one that arrives unexpectedly when something beautiful touches a part of you that words cannot fully reach.
A song that finds an old ache.A room that feels familiar before you understand why.A place that quietly reminds you that you belong.
That is the feeling we have spent years searching for.
That pause.That stillness.That shiver.
And though no place can promise transformation, we can say this:
For more than twenty years, people have left Oasis carrying something they did not arrive with.
Something quieter.Something softer.Something entirely their own.
Shivers is not merely a place you visit.
It is a place that remains with you long after you have gone.

The Heart of Goa
Has Been Waiting
For over two decades, this land has held stories—of wild escapes, quiet awakenings, and the enduring human need to belong.Rooted in the heart of Goa, our journey began twenty years ago, when the essence of this place still whispered softly, untouched by the rush of the world.
The Promise
Long before Goa became a destination, it was a feeling.
And twenty years ago, we made a quiet promise—not to build the grandest retreat, nor to chase fleeting definitions of luxury—but to create a space that holds something far more meaningful.
A refuge for those who arrive carrying the weight of the world.For those who have forgotten the sound of stillness.For those whose bodies remained in the city, while their spirit searched for something slower, deeper, more real.
We found it here—in the heart of Goa.Not the Goa of neon and noise, but the other one.The one that wakes before the world does.
Where mornings carry the scent of earth after rain.Where coconut palms move gently, as though they understand time differently.Where life is not measured, but felt.
And for twenty years, this is what we have carefully, quietly nurtured.
What People Find Here
Guests arrive seeking escape.What they discover is something far more profound.
A softened rhythm.A deeper breath.A return to a self they had almost forgotten.
We have watched hurried lives slow down over unhurried meals.We have seen strangers become family beneath open skies and shared silence.We have witnessed moments of quiet release—tears not of sorrow, but of relief.
These are the moments that define us.Not accolades or rankings—but the quiet recognition in someone’s eyes when they remember who they are.
What We Are
We are not defined by luxury in its conventional sense.What we offer is rarer—something grounded, sincere, and enduring.
A place where the air feels untouched,where food is honest and rooted in the land,and where time unfolds at its own pace.
Here, tradition and modernity do not compete—they coexist.The warmth of Goan heritage, the depth of its flavours, and the ease of contemporary living come together effortlessly.
There are no grand facades.No rehearsed gestures.
Only the glow of firelight against timeworn walls,a meal shaped by the earth it came from,and long, unclaimed hours that belong entirely to you.
Who This Is For
This space is not meant for everyone.
For those seeking constant motion, noise, and distraction, the world offers endless alternatives.
But if somewhere within you there remains a quiet memory—of clean air, of stillness, of something unfiltered and real—then perhaps you already understand.
Perhaps you have been finding your way here all along.

The Northeast Tiffin Box Goa
An Awakening of Forgotten Flavours & Distant Lands
Where the mountains of the Northeast meet the warmth of Goa’s table, a quiet journey begins.
Across mist-wrapped hills, ancient valleys, and lands shaped by fire, rain, and memory, a cuisine was born — one that speaks softly through smoke, earth, spice, and slow time. The Northeast Tiffin Box carries this essence across distance, arriving gently into Goa’s culinary landscape like a story waiting to be heard.
It comes not to overwhelm, but to invite.To awaken curiosity.To offer something beautifully unfamiliar.
Each tiffin carries far more than a meal.It holds memory — of recipes preserved through generations, of open flames burning patiently through long evenings, of ingredients deeply rooted in season, soil, and ancestry.
What Lives Within
🔥 SmokeSlow fire. Ancient technique. Flavours shaped with patience — never rushed, never replicated.
🌿 EarthIngredients connected intimately to land and season. Honest. Untouched. Deeply rooted in origin.
🏔 MemoryRecipes older than menus themselves. Heritage carried carefully forward in every spoonful.
The flavours reveal themselves slowly — layered, soulful, unfolding with quiet confidence. They do not announce themselves loudly; they linger gently, leaving behind more than satisfaction alone.
They leave discovery.
The quiet feeling that you have tasted something entirely new, and yet somehow familiar to the soul.
An Invitation, Never A Transaction
This is an experience shaped for the curious.For those willing to wander beyond the expected and discover beauty within simplicity.
Within every offering lives a quiet boldness — a confidence born not from performance, but from authenticity. From heritage. From a profound connection to origin that remains beautifully intact despite the distance travelled.
“We do not simply serve food.We serve the feeling of arriving somewhere you have never been — and recognising it anyway.”
The Northeast Tiffin Box exists as a passage —
Between regions.Between cultures.Between the familiar and the yet-to-be discovered.
And somewhere along that journey, a new appreciation begins to take shape — not only for a cuisine, but for the people behind it, the mountains it came from, and the quiet act of carrying something precious across great distance to place it, warmly and humbly, upon another table.
This is not fast food.This is not novelty disguised as fusion.
This is memory made portable.Culture made warm.Tradition carried with intention.
Every tiffin arrives carrying the full weight of where it comes from — with honesty, warmth, and soul untouched.
These are not merely flavours.
These are feelings, gently served at your door.
